🍦 Scoop Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Scoop ("we", "us", "our") operates the Scoop app, which connects customers to nearby ice cream vans. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address, and if you sign in with Google or Facebook, basic profile details such as your name.
  • Role information: whether you're using Scoop as a customer or as a driver, and your van name if you're a driver.
  • Location data: if you're a customer, the location you choose (via GPS or address search) when requesting a van. If you're a driver, your live location while you're marked "live" on the map. Location shown to other users is approximate, not exact.
  • Saved home address: if you choose to save a home address for convenience, we store its coordinates against your account.
  • Payment information: if you sign up as a driver, subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details ourselves — Stripe handles this directly.

2. How we use your information

  • To show nearby vans to customers, and nearby demand to drivers.
  • To operate your account and remember your role and preferences.
  • To process driver subscription payments via Stripe.
  • To send account-related emails (e.g. password resets, sign-up confirmation).

3. How long we keep your data

Location requests are intended to be shown to drivers for around 30 minutes, after which they're no longer displayed on the map. However, request records may remain stored in our database beyond this window until removed by a cleanup process or by you cancelling the request. Live driver location is updated continuously while you're "live" and stops being shared once you go offline. Account and saved home address data is kept until you delete your account or ask us to remove it.

4. Who we share data with

  • Supabase — our backend and database provider, which stores account, location, and request data.
  • Stripe — processes driver subscription payments.
  • Google / Facebook — if you choose to sign in using these services, they share basic profile information with us as part of the login process.
  • Google Maps — used to display the map and power address search.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not currently use third-party analytics or advertising tracking tools.

5. Your choices

  • You can cancel an active location request at any time from the app.
  • Drivers can go offline at any time to stop sharing their live location.
  • You can ask us to delete your account and associated data by contacting us (see below).

6. Children

Scoop is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Scoop after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at info.scoop67@gmail.com.
